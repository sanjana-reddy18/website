JOB TITLES

CONSTRUCTION PROJECT MANAGER

A construction project manager is an expert who plans, directs, and monitors a construction project. They collaborate with engineers and architects to establish a schedule, allocate resources, and guarantee project completion on schedule. Planning, employing, terminating, and managing staff are among their responsibilities.

COST ESTIMATOR

Cost estimators gather information and evaluate it to determine how much money, time, labor, and materials will be needed to produce a good, build a structure, or offer a service. Typically, they focus on a specific sector or product.
